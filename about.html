About Code Combat

Who We Are

Code Combat is an interactive online platform that gamifies the learning of coding. Our mission is to make coding education accessible, engaging, and fun for learners of all ages and skill levels. Through our carefully designed games, we aim to inspire a passion for Javascript programming and equip learners with the skills they need to thrive in the digital world.

How It Works

Code Combat offers a unique learning experience where players embark on coding adventures in a fantasy world. By writing real code in a programming language of their choice, such as Python, JavaScript, or Java, players control their characters and navigate through challenging levels. They can learn and practice key coding concepts, problem-solving skills, and algorithmic thinking while having a great time.

Our mission

At Code Combat, our mission is to empower learners to become proficient coders through an immersive and enjoyable learning journey. We believe that learning to code should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or prior knowledge. We strive to create a supportive community where learners can collaborate, share ideas, and celebrate their coding accomplishments.
